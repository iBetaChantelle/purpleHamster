About Us

Do you hear the bells on Sunday Morning?"

Celebrating over 95 years in Aurora, AFCC located across from the Aurora City Park at the corner of 1601 Dayton St. was founded in 1924 and rebuilt following the fire in 1958. This friendly neighborhood Disciples of Christ Church has also been host to several other congregations of different denominations over the past few decades. Over the decades this church has changed but persists as a welcoming presence in the community.

We welcome all visitors from all aspects of life to join us for Worship services every Sunday at 10:15AM. The attire is "come as you are", communion is open to everyone, and offering is not required.

We are currently broadcasting something new each Sunday on Facebook
